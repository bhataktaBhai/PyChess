Pieces are indicated by the first letter of their names in uppercase, except for the Knight.
King - K
Queen - Q
Rook - R
Bishop - B
Knight - N (since K is already allotted to the King)
Pawns are denoted not by a letter, but by the absence of any.

A move with the White King from d3 to c4 would be written as:
    Kc4
Note that the starting square is not mentioned and there are no spaces.
However, either of the starting co-ordinates may be added to make the move clearer.
    Kdc4, K3c4, Kd3c4
